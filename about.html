About Piash Chowdhury

I am Piash Chowdhury, a Senior GIS Analyst with expertise in hydrodynamic modelling, geospatial analysis, remote sensing, and machine learning. I hold degrees in Geography and Environment from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology, Bangladesh. My research and professional experience focus on environmental modeling, hydrodynamic simulations, and applying AI to natural hazard assessment. My goal is to advance sustainable solutions through geospatial technology and AI is environmental Modelling and Water resources Managment.
